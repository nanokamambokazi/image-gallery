KwaZulu Natal


Home page

KwaZulu Natal is aprovince of South Africa that was created in 1994 when the Zulu bantustan of KwaZulu("place of the Zulu") and Natal province were merged. It located in the southeast of the country and it capital is Pietermaritzburg and its largest city is Durban. It is also the 2nd most populous province in South Africa.
