Things you should Do in South Africa

  1. Try all the food.
  2. Shop the street markets.
  3. Do take the taxis.
  4. Rent a car.
  5. Lock your car door and close the windows when travelling to the big city.

Things you should NOT do in South Africa

  1. Don't pronounce Zebra wrong.
  2. Don't underestimate the vastness of the country.
  3. Don't expect to see wild animals everywhere you go.
  4. Don't forget to tip.
  5. Don't flash expensive electronics and jewellery.
  6. Don't only visit top tourist destinations.
  7. Don't assume that people dont speak English.
