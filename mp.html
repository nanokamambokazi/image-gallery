Mpumalanga


Home Page

Mpumalanga is a province of South Africa. The name means "the place where the sun rises" and it's capital is Mbombela(Nelspruit). It embraces the southern half of Kruger National Park, a huge nature reserve with abundant wildlife including big game. The province's iconic Blyde River Canyon, among the world's largest ,is known as a green canyon because of it's subtropical foliage.

Things to do in Mpumalanga
